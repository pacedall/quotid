Privacy

Last updated: · This is a starting template — have it reviewed before relying on it.

Quotid is a daily puzzle site operated by Pacedall Labs. This page explains what we collect and the choices you have. Questions: team@pacedall.com.

What we collect

  • If you create an account: your email address, a securely hashed password (never stored in plain text), and your puzzle play history (dates, scores, streaks).
  • If you play without an account: your progress is stored only in your own browser — we don't receive it.
  • Analytics: we use Plausible, a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics tool that records aggregate visits only. It does not track you across sites or build a profile.
  • Cookies: managed via the consent banner. We keep these to a minimum.

Why, and our legal basis

We use your email to run your account, secure it (verification, password reset), and — only if you leave reminders on — to let you know a new puzzle is ready. The legal basis is performance of our service to you and your consent for reminders, which you can withdraw at any time.

How long we keep it

We keep your account data until you delete your account. Verification and reset links expire automatically (7 days and 1 hour respectively).
